About Me

I am 28 years old, and have lived in Richmond all my life. I have vast experience in child care and development programs, not just as a counselor, but as a manager and operations director as well throughout the past 8 years.

I also had the pleasure of being a soccer coach for my former high school. At the end of my 4-year tenure, I had helped take a program that finished with 1 win in 2013 to 10 wins, including 3 playoff wins, in 2015.

I believe that these two jobs have given me an incredible amount of experience, stories, and life lessons that will stick with me forever

In my free time, you can find me watching soccer or football, playing with my niece, or hanging out with my dog, Chelsea.
